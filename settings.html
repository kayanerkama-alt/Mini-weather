📖 How the Garden Works

Your virtual garden grows based on real weather conditions at your location:


🌡️ Temperature — Ideal range: 15–25°C (25 pts)

💧 Humidity — Ideal range: 40–70% (25 pts)

💨 Wind — Calm winds below 15 km/h (20 pts)

🌧️ Precipitation — No rain is best (15 pts)

☀️ UV Index — Safe UV below 3 (15 pts)


Score 88+ = 🌻 Thriving | 68+ = 🌿 Healthy | 45+ = 🌾 Stressed | Below = 🍂 Wilted


The garden resets monthly. Check in daily to build your streak!
